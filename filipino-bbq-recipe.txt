Nuwave Filipino Barbecue

Recipe modified from Pinch of Yum: http://pinchofyum.com/filipino-chicken-barbecue

Ingredients:
 - 3/4 tsp garlic granules
 - 1 tsp black pepper
 - 2 tbsp lime juice
 - 2 tbsp oyster sauce
 - 1 tbsp soy sauce
 - 1/4 tsp salt
 - 2 tbsp brown sugar
 - 2 lbs boneless skinless chicken thighs
 - Splash of olive oil


Directions:
 1. Combine all ingredients except chicken and olive oil in a Ziploc bag. Place the chicken in the bag and let it marinate for at least 4 hours (best if overnight).
 2. Add a splash of olive oil to the bag and squish it throughout the bag carefully to distribute it. Let the bag rest for about 5-10 minutes.
 3. Place the chicken on the Nuwave rack and cook for 12 minutes on each side.
 4. Serve with rice.
